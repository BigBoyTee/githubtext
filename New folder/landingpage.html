ParentHub

Welcome to ParentHub
your trusted online marketplace for parents to donate, exchange, or sell gently used items their kids have outgrown. At ParentHub, we believe in giving new life to pre-loved children's items, making it easy for you to declutter and help other families in need. Join our community today and discover a convenient way to recycle quality kids' items while supporting sustainable parenting.

Services

Donate

This service enables you to give surplus products that your kids no longer need nor uses to a parent who has a kid who might be in need of that product but they cannot afford them.

Exchange

This service will help you to exchange whatever your kids no longer user with what thy might need with another person who has what you and you have what you need. this services will require bidding and negotiation between both parties

Sell

In this categories, you will be able to list items for sale and they have to meet our requirement. in this categories negotiation is allowed for both parties and exchange with addition of some cash is allowed.
